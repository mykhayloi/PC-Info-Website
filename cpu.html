CPU

CPUs are much less important when it comes to building gaming PCs. There are however a couple of things you should look out for. First of all, look out for compatibility. Unlike with GPUs not all motherboards fit all CPUs. Make sure that your CPU fits into your motherboard. You should try and buy your pieces to all be roughly the same quality to not waste money.
